My Hobbies

I only have a few hobbies during my free time, here are all of my Hobbies.

First: GYM

Going to the gym isn't just a workout for me — it's my daily reset. Lifting weights, running on the treadmill, or doing functional training helps me clear my mind, build resilience, and feel alive. It's not about looking perfect; it's about feeling stronger than yesterday.

Second: Cosplay

Cosplay lets me step into the shoes of my favorite characters. I enjoy making costumes, attending conventions, and connecting with other fans who share the same passion. It's creativity, confidence, and community all in one.

Third: Gaming

I love gaming because it lets me escape into different worlds and have fun with friends. From action-packed battles to relaxing building games, there's always something new to try. It's my favorite way to relax and stay connected.
